                II.    RATIONALE
                       Education has a predominant role in fulfilling our aspirations in life may it be through inclusive
                and special needs education and whether it is in a public or private institution. Its main agenda is to
                make us knowledgeable and less ignorant. The growth of social awareness on the plight of the less
                fortunate persons with disabilities has led the government to facilitate opportunities for them to develop
                their full potential in school especially in inclusive education so that they can live independently while
                enjoying  their  rights  and  privileges  as  human  beings.  As  we  give  value  to  diversity  along  with
                considering the uniqueness of everyone, this will forge strong bonds to aid in the exploration of one's
                passion, talent, and capabilities.
                       As we grow and blend in the information age, it is also right to consider adapting technology-
                based  teaching  and learning  to  special and inclusive education.  Digital  technology  has  a  potential
                impact for a wider access to learning and may open doors to new teaching strategies. The goal of this
                webinar is to promote the utilization of technology-based teaching strategy to promote quality, relevant,
                                              st
                and effective instruction for the 21  century learners. Furthermore, this webinar aims to recognize the
                highlight of the current inclusive practices and role of digital technologies in special education. As we
                reach for our goals in life, this webinar will serve as a reminder that we are all capable if we have the
                eagerness to discover our potential with appreciation towards others regardless of all our differences.

                III.   OBJECTIVES
                                          This activity aims to:
                                   1.  examine the methods that would foster comprehensive education;
                                   2.  set perception on the essence of respect and acceptance, inside and outside
                                      the classroom;
                                   3.  discuss the concept of unity in existing diversity by applying essential and
                                      ensuring teaching strategies;
                                   4.  recognize the highlight of the current inclusive practices and role of digital
                                      technologies in special education;
                                   5.  promote  the  advantages  of  using  digital  technologies  as  part  of  inclusive
                                      practices to support learners; and
                                   6.  provide  useful  guidance  for  conducting  developmental  assessments  and
                                      inclusive activities.
